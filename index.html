Projects and Applications:
Dilemmas (2017)

Mobile App for iOS and Android using Ionic framework.

Ever find yourself struggling to decide what food to eat? Which clothes to wear? Struggle no more. With Dilemmas, you can put your problem forth and have your peers vote on how to resolve your dilemma!

Forking Great Recipes (2017)

Recipe scraper Web App for recipes developed using MEAN stack

Do you find it hard to keep track of multiple recipes from popular sites? Do you ever make changes to the recipes you use but forget the amount of ingredients that work best for you? Forking Great Recipes is a recipe scraper that scrapes the content from popular recipe websites and adds them to your own person/editable page. Sites supported: Taste, Delicious
